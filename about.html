About Us

Our History

We have been serving our community since 1966. At Prosperity Bank, we are committed to providing exceptional financial services that cater to the diverse needs of our community. Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and businesses to achieve their financial goals with confidence and ease. Prosperity Bank has grown from a small local bank into a trusted financial institution serving hundreds of customers. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a bank that offers personalized service, innovative solutions, and unwavering integrity. Over the years, we have stayed true to this vision, continually evolving to meet the changing needs of our clients and the financial landscape.
